FAQ

The usual questions.

Do I need to prepare anything?

Just the pre-session brief. You don't need to build a presentation or gather a stack of reports beforehand.

Is this a sales call for a bigger project?

No. If you decide afterward that you want more help, we can talk about it, but that isn't the purpose of the session.

What if 60 minutes isn't enough?

It usually is. If something genuinely needs another session, I'll say so clearly and we can book a follow-up at the same rate.

Do you work with businesses outside Canada?

Yes. Sessions are remote, so I work with clients across Canada and the U.S.

What's your cancellation policy?

Reschedule at least 24 hours in advance and there's no problem. Cancellations or reschedule requests inside that 24-hour window are charged 50% of the session fee. No-shows are charged the full CA$175 + tax. Once the session has happened, there are no refunds.
