The process behind every project.

A simple five-phase framework that combines strategic planning with hands-on execution, so you get the marketing results you asked for without the overwhelm.

Phase 01 / Discovery

Listen and research

I start by understanding your brand, audience, competitors, and goals. Every recommendation that follows is built on real research, not guesswork.

Phase 02 / Strategy

Map the plan

A clear, documented strategy sets the direction: what we'll create, why, for whom, and how success will be measured before any work begins.

Phase 03 / Execution

Write and build

Precise deliverables, reviewed and refined through structured feedback rounds until every detail is right.

Phase 04 / Launch

Review and refine

Once your project goes live, we review early performance against the benchmarks set in Phase 02 and make adjustments before scaling up.

Phase 05 / Ongoing

Optimize and grow

Marketing is never finished. I continue optimizing based on real performance data to keep you competitive as the market shifts.
