Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This page explains what information Sipply Consulting collects when you use this website, why it's collected, and how it's handled. This site is a small business website with no user accounts, no e-commerce, and no advertising trackers.

Information you provide directly

When you submit the contact or consultation form on this site, you may provide your name, email address, company or website (optional), and a message describing your project. This information is sent through Formspree, a third-party form-processing service, and delivered to Sipply Consulting's inbox.

This information is used only to respond to your inquiry or schedule a consultation. It is not sold, rented, or shared with any other third party for marketing purposes.

Information collected automatically

This site does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. A single preference — light or dark mode — may be stored locally in your browser so the site remembers your display setting. This preference never leaves your device and is not linked to your identity.

Standard web server logs (such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited) may be collected by the hosting provider for security and performance purposes. These logs are not used to build a profile of individual visitors.

Third-party services

  • Formspree — processes form submissions. See Formspree's privacy policy for details on how they handle submitted data.
  • Hosting provider — serves the website files and may log basic connection data as described above.

Data retention

Messages submitted through the contact or consultation form are kept only as long as needed to respond to the inquiry or maintain a record of the working relationship, and are deleted upon request.

Your rights

You can ask what information is held about you, request a copy of it, ask for it to be corrected, or request its deletion at any time by contacting the email address below.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for business use and is not directed at children. It does not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in how the site operates. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy or how your information is handled can be sent through the consultation page or directly by email.
