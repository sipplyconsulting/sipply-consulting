Every prompt has a footprint.

Behind every AI query is a real power plant burning fuel, a real grid straining under load, and real water disappearing from real rivers. This isn't abstract — it's happening right now, to feed machines that answer questions a search engine could answer for a fraction of the cost.

03 — The reversal is already happening

Companies are rehiring the humans they replaced.

The rush to replace people with AI is colliding with reality. Quality drops, customers revolt, and companies are quietly walking it back.

Klarna

Replaced roughly 700 customer service employees with AI. Service quality declined, customers revolted, and the company had to rehire humans.12

29% and rising

Share of companies that made AI-related layoffs and have already rehired staff back, according to Forbes reporting on 2026 workforce data.13

Only 9%

of executives, managers and workers surveyed said they'd want to replace their entire workforce with AI tools.11
